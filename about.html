ABOUT VERDAIA

Verdaia Consulting Ltd. is a Zambian-based, globally connected consulting firm founded in 2024 with a strategic presence in Zambia & the Netherlands.

Specializing in Research, Development, & Consultancy, Verdaia Consulting Ltd. delivers innovative & sustainable solutions across Life & Health Sciences, Engineering, Climate Change & the Environment. The company earns its reputation for driving innovation & delivering impactful results through strategic partnerships & a commitment to sustainable development.
